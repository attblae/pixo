Where Every Stroke Finds a Home

Welcome to PIXO, a premier digital marketplace and auction house dedicated exclusively to the world of hand-drawn art. We believe that every drawing tells a story, and our mission is to ensure those stories are heard, seen, and valued.

Our Mission

At PIXO, we bridge the gap between talented independent artists and passionate art enthusiasts. We’ve created a space where creativity meets opportunity, allowing artists to showcase their unique visions while giving collectors the chance to own one-of-a-kind original works.

For Artists

We provide a platform built for creators. Whether you are a professional illustrator or an emerging talent, our site offers you the tools to reach a global audience. Through our auction system, your work finds its true market value, driven by the appreciation of real fans and collectors.

For Collectors

Searching for something truly unique? Our curated auctions bring you the best in contemporary drawing. From intricate sketches to bold masterpieces, you can bid on exclusive pieces that resonate with your soul. We ensure a transparent and exciting bidding process, making art acquisition an experience to remember.
